Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a traditional Indian dish consisting of chicken prepared with an array of spices. It is complimented by gravy, and typically served on top of a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons ground cumin, divided
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided, or more to taste
  • 3 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 long skewers
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 can of tomato sauce, 8 ounces
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Combine yogurt, lemon juice, 2 teaspoons cumin, cinnamon, cayenne, black pepper, ginger, and 1 teaspoon salt in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in chicken, cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour
  3. Preheat grill for high heat
  4. Lightly oil grill grate, thread chicken onto skewers, and discard marinade
  5. Grill chicken until juices run clear, about 5 minutes per side
  6. Melt butter in large heavy skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic and jalapeno for 1 minute. Season with remaining 2 teaspoons cumin, paprika and remaining 1 teaspoon salt. Stir in tomato sauce and cream. Simmer on low heat until sauce thickens, about 20 minutes.
  7. Add grilled chicken and simmer for 10 minites. Transfer to serving platter and garnish with fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve over rice.
