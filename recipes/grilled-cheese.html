Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Delicious, melted cheese sandwiched between two slices of your favorite bread. Cooked to perfection, it pairs well with a side of tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 1-3 slices of American Cheese
  • 2 slices of bread, white
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the bread and place one slice butter side down on a hot skillet
  2. Place cheese on top
  3. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter side up
  4. Cook until bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip
  5. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted
